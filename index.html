Defibrillator Projects

Saving lives across Doncaster

Sudden Cardiac Arrest can happen anywhere, anytime. DCD places publicly accessible defibrillators (AEDs) across Doncaster — in iconic red phone boxes, community centres, and public spaces — giving everyone the best possible chance when seconds count.

Volunteer Training
Teaching locals to use defibrillators confidently during emergencies — knowledge saves lives
Community Access
Placing AEDs in public spots for quick, lifesaving response — 24/7 accessible
YAS Registered
Registered with Yorkshire Ambulance Service so dispatchers can direct callers to our devices
Fully Funded
100% funded by community donations — every pound goes directly to purchasing certified AEDs

Ramadhan Food Packs

Uniting locals with care & respect

We unite locals to provide Ramadhan food packs with care and respect. Driven by volunteers, every donation goes directly to those in need — ensuring no family goes without during the blessed month.

Sponsor a Family
01
Essential Food Hampers

Halal staples, rice, pulses, cooking oil, dates and essentials providing nutritious Suhoor and Iftar meals. Every pack assembled with care by our volunteers.

02
Volunteer Help

Community members come together to prepare and deliver food packs with kindness — our packing days are a community event in themselves.

03
Home Delivery

For elderly, disabled or vulnerable families unable to collect, our volunteers deliver directly to the door — because dignity matters in how we give.

04
Community Iftar

We organise community Iftar gatherings bringing Doncaster's Muslim community together to break fast and strengthen bonds of brotherhood and sisterhood.

05
Zakat & Sadaqah Eligible

100% of food pack donations reach those in need. Your Zakat and Sadaqah are handled with full accountability and transparency.

06
Ramadhan Support

Providing food packs to families in need during Ramadhan with care and respect. Nutritious essentials carefully selected to support fasting families throughout Ramadhan.

"The best charity is that given in Ramadhan — every pack carries a prayer for the giver and warmth for the receiver."

Homeless Food Share

Our Mission — No one goes hungry

Supporting those in need with food, care, and community spirit. Our Homeless Food Share initiative provides regular, warm, nutritious meals and essential care packages to those sleeping rough or in temporary accommodation across Doncaster.

Donation Impact
Every penny goes directly to those in need — fresh, nutritious meals shared daily with care and compassion
Meal Distribution
Regular hot meals distributed at community locations across Doncaster town centre — no questions asked
Volunteer Support
Community members joining hands to serve — more than food, we sit, listen, and see the person
Winter Essentials
Sleeping bags, warm clothing, gloves and hygiene packs distributed during cold months
Signposting Services
Connecting rough sleepers with local housing, shelter and healthcare resources
Join Us: We're always looking for volunteers to help with meal prep and distribution, and donations of tinned food, warm clothing, and hygiene products. Every contribution changes lives.
